Our Work Process Before starting a renovation project, first evaluate your location and understand your needs. We assess the overall condition of your property and provide practical recommendations for optimal refurbishment of your desired area. We offer free offers to create a budget using survey or assessment results. It also provides detailed information about the scope of work and the time frame of the project. We comply with Malaysian regulations to ensure that projects in Malaysia meet all local, state and national regulations.
